# Test Sample Specifications (City Block)

## 1. Street Widths
| Type | Real World | Model Scale (1:5,533) | Laser Cutter Implications |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Main Street** | ~10m (33 ft) | ~1.8 mm | Clearly visible channel. |
| **Secondary** | ~5m (16 ft) | ~0.9 mm | Fine line. |
| **Alley** | ~2.5m (8 ft) | ~0.45 mm | Hairline/Score line. May be limit of material resolution. |

## 2. Building Rules
### Rich Housing
*   **Dimensions:** ~15m x 15m (~2.7mm).
*   **Shapes:**
    *   **Square (O-Type):** Thicker walls (30-40% of size) for substantial look.
    *   **L-Type & U-Type:** Random Orientation (0, 90, 180, 270 deg).
    *   **Z-Type:** Included for variety.
    *   **8-Type:** Double courtyard.

### Poor Housing
*   **Dimensions:** ~5m x 6m (~1mm x 1.1mm).
*   **Spacing:** **Standard Scale Gap + 1px** (Essential for definition at 1K res).
*   **Distribution:**
    *   **Missing:** 20% chance of empty lot.
    *   **Coalesced:** 30% chance of merging adjacent units (Slum blocks).

## 3. Sample Size
*   **Dimensions:** 40mm x 40mm (4cm x 4cm).
*   **Resolution:** 1K (to prevent melting).
